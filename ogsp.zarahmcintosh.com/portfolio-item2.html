Stormy

Stormy won her first major at 6 months of age. She has the attitude, the elegant body and the smooth moving gait of a worthy champion. Before winning her championship, Stormy wins Best Of Breed over Specials (Finished show Champions being campaigned around the country) twice in a row in Idaho, while handled by myself, a very novice handler! Stormy finished at the GSP Club of WA Specialty on Dec 4th, 2009, at only 2 weeks past her year birthday. During that same show, her brother, Khan, takes his first Best In Show Specialty after only his fourth time being shown as a special. What a way to finish, sharing wins with her brother, Khan!
